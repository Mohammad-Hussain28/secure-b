HOME LOAN

Over View


A home loan, also known as a mortgage, this is the most common type of home loan availed to purchase a house.

It is a financial product that enables individuals to purchase or refinance real estate by borrowing money from a lender.

The borrower agrees to repay the loan amount along with interest over a specified period.

The lender will hold the house until you completely repay the loan.

Types Of Home Loans

Fixed Rate Mortgage (FRM) :

Features a constant interest rate throughout the loan term. Predictable monthly payments, providing stability and budgeting ease.

Adjustable-Rate Mortgage (ARM):

Interest rates may fluctuate based on market conditions. Initial lower rates but subject to change, often after an initial fixed period.

Federal Housing Administration(FHA):

Government-backed loans with lower down payment requirements. Suitable for first-time homebuyers or those with limited finances.

Benifits Of Home Loan

Tax Benifits :

Certain home loan components, such as principal and interest repayments, may be eligible for tax deductions.

Insurance Options :

Lenders often provide insurance options like home loan insurance to protect against unforeseen circumstances.

EMI Options :

Equated Monthly Installments (EMIs) can be customized based on income and financial situations.

Part-payment Facility :

Some loans permit partial repayment of the principal amount, reducing the overall interest burden.

Caution

It's crucial to carefully review and compare these features from various lenders to choose a home loan that aligns with your financial goals and preferences.
